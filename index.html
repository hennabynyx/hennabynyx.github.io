Services

Here are the diversified services that I provide with henna art. I am constantly traveling to events and parks to showcase and spread awareness about henna as an art form.

Bridal

Bridal events are the most popular way of expression of henna art. It is of huge significance in Indian weddings. It is perfect for a fusion themed wedding!

Parties & Events

Henna is a fun way to create a twist to conventional birthday parties or any other event by giving the attendees something memorable to take with them.

Private Appointments

Henna is a medium to get temporary tattoos on yourself when you are attending any event or going on a trip and it is sure to turn the spotlight on you.
